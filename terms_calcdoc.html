1. The "Zero-Knowledge" Privacy Policy

CalcDoc is built on the principle of Data Sovereignty. This means you own your data, and we‚Äîthe developers‚Äîhave absolutely no way to see, touch, or access it.

1.1 No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect any Personal Identifiable Information (PII). This includes, but is not limited to: Names, Email addresses, Phone numbers, Device IDs, or IP addresses. Since there is no signup process, your identity remains anonymous.

1.2 Local Storage Only

All files (PDFs, Images, Documents) that you add to CalcDoc are stored directly on your physical device. We do not use servers. We do not use third-party cloud providers (like AWS or Google Cloud) to store your secrets.

1.3 Encryption & Password Security

Your "Equation Password" is the only key to your vault.

  • Local Verification: Your password logic is stored locally in a secure, hashed format.
  • No Backdoors: Because we have no server access, we cannot "reset" your password. If the equation is forgotten, the data remains encrypted and inaccessible.
